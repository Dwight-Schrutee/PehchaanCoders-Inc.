About

About Us

Pehchaan aims the following

  • Ease in Identification
  • Fast Approval by AICTE and MHRD
  • Ease in Distribution of Fellowships, Grants and Training.

Every year more than 6 crores of indians graduates from diverse backgrounds and with diverse backgrounds and with diversity in education. Almost similar number of students enter into the colleges for taking various education to help them in seeking jobs. The task to track studemts on a one on one basis is also hectic due to the large number. AICTE and MHRD aims to track students and halp them in various processes like ease of student identification, ease of AICTE approved college tracking, ease in distribution of fellowship grants, training. since there is no one unique identification of students the process itself takes time as it requires to have letters from college heads which is also time taking process. Hence to track huge number manually nis hectic and a time taking process. Pehchaan targets such an issue.

Benefits

Ease in Identification Now, students can save themselves from acquiring college heads manually and directly get them verified on our website.

Fast Approval by AICTE and MHRD The officials of their respective departments can view the student details and documents and get them approved much much more quickly.

Simple and Quick way for university officials College officials can now easily upload the letter heads and other documents for a particular student.

Ease in Distribution of Fellowships, Grants and Training.
